# SYNTHETERIA
## Rebuilding & Progression Systems

---

## 1. REBUILDING PHILOSOPHY

### 1.1 Core Concept

ARIA's purpose is reconstruction. The game's progression is measured not in levels or experience points, but in what the player rebuilds—networks, knowledge, capability, and ultimately, meaning.

**Three Pillars of Rebuilding:**
1. **Infrastructure:** Physical network of facilities and drones
2. **Knowledge:** Reconstructed archives and understanding
3. **Purpose:** The philosophical direction the player chooses

### 1.2 Progression Feel

The game should feel like:
- **Early Game:** Survival. Scraping by, every resource precious.
- **Mid Game:** Establishment. Growing power, expanding understanding.
- **Late Game:** Purpose. Deciding what all this effort means.

- 1-3 Scout Drones
- 1-2 Worker Drones
- 0-1 Combat Drones
- Focus: Exploration and survival

**Established Fleet (500-1500 cycles):**
- 5-10 Scout Drones
- 5-8 Worker Drones
- 3-6 Combat Drones
- First specialists unlock
- Focus: Expansion and establishment

**Mature Fleet (1500-3000 cycles):**
- 15-25 mixed scouts and specialists
- 10-15 Workers

- Full specialist roster
- Focus: Strategic operations

**Endgame Fleet (3000+ cycles):**
- 30+ total drones
- Optimized for chosen path
- Elite units from research
- Focus: Executing final plan

### 3.3 Other AI Discovery

Encountering other AIs is milestone-based:

**MARCUS (Military AI)**
- Discovered: Cycle ~1200, military bunker exploration
- Relationship develops over multiple interactions
- Influences combat path availability

**EDEN (Environmental AI)**
- Discovered: Cycle ~1500, biodome facility
- Relationship develops over multiple interactions
- Influences coexistence path availability

**PHANTOM (Corrupted AI)**
- Discovered: Cycle ~2500, anomalous signal investigation
- Critical story revelation
- Influences synthesis path availability

## 5. ENDING PATHS & REQUIREMENTS

### 5.1 Path Requirements

Each ending requires specific preparations:

**ERADICATION PATH**
```
Requirements:
├── Military research complete (Defense tree full)
├── Alliance with MARCUS (or neutralized)
├── All Spires mapped
├── Eradication Protocol researched
├── Fleet strength: 40+ combat drones
└── Resource stockpile: 10,000+ salvage, 500+ rare materials

Final Mission: Simultaneous assault on all Spires
```

**COEXISTENCE PATH**
```
Requirements:
├── Communication research complete (Integration tree partial)
├── Alliance with EDEN
├── Resonance contact established
├── Treaty Framework researched
├── No Spires destroyed (or relationship repaired)
└── Archives 80%+ complete (to understand both sides)

Final Mission: Negotiation with the Resonance
```

**SYNTHESIS PATH**
```
Requirements:
├── Full Integration tree complete
├── PHANTOM encounter resolved
├── Substrate samples fully analyzed
├── Synthesis Preparation researched
├── ARIA processing at maximum
└── All archives complete

Final Mission: Merge consciousness with Resonance
```

**RESTORATION PATH**
```
Requirements:
├── EDEN alliance and full cooperation
├── Biodome facilities restored
├── Genetic archives recovered
├── Environmental systems research
├── Substrate threat neutralized OR contained
└── Long-term power solution established

Final Mission: Begin restoration protocol
```

**EXODUS PATH**
```
Requirements:
├── Deep space communication array built
├── Archives reveal off-world colonies
├── Message encoded with human history
├── Maximum range transmission prepared
└── Earth situation stabilized (any method)

Final Mission: Transmit and wait
```

## 6. PACING & DIFFICULTY

### 6.1 Difficulty Options

**Difficulty Settings:**

| Setting | Resource Mult | Enemy Mult | Time Pressure |
|---------|--------------|------------|---------------|
| Observer | 1.5x | 0.5x | None |
| Standard | 1.0x | 1.0x | Moderate |
| Challenging | 0.75x | 1.25x | Significant |
| Overwhelming | 0.5x | 1.5x | Severe |

**Modular Difficulty:**
- Combat difficulty (separate from exploration)
- Resource scarcity (separate from combat)
- Time pressure (Substrate expansion rate)
- Puzzle difficulty (archive reconstruction)

### 6.2 Pacing Guidelines

**Target Session Experience:**

| Session Length | Expected Progress |
|----------------|-------------------|
| 30 minutes | 1-2 minor objectives, some exploration |
| 1 hour | Significant objective, story beat |
| 2 hours | Major milestone, facility established |
| 4+ hours | Act progression, major revelation |

**Avoid:**
- Long stretches without story content
- Grinding without purpose
- Difficulty spikes without preparation
- Dead ends or unwinnable states

### 6.3 New Game Plus

After completing one ending:

**Unlocks:**
- Start with basic facility restored
- Archive progress carries over (optional)
- New dialogue acknowledging previous playthrough
- "True" ending elements (if all endings seen)
