---

## Previous Balance Findings (Still Applicable)

These observations from the old builds should inform the redesign:

- **Power system works well**: All builds achieved sustainable power ratios
- **Compute system is balanced**: Combat units can be net contributors (good design)
- **Automation multipliers are impactful**: Moving from simple to adaptive creates meaningful trade-offs
- **Legs need proportionally more torque**: Intentional balance decision
- **Cargo haulers are sluggish when loaded**: Realistic and intentional
- **Heavy Motor gap**: Needed a motor between Large (50 torque) and Industrial (200 torque) — Heavy Motor at 100 torque filled this
