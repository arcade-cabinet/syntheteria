## The New Earth

Earth is **fully mechanized**, governed entirely by the sentient EL. The EL are not mysterious cosmic villains — they arrived, settled into Earth's AI infrastructure, and simply... run things now.

**Humanity is irrelevant.** Not extinct. Not enslaved. Not at war. Humans are to the EL what ants are to us — background noise doing whatever it is humans do. There was no great war. No genocide. No cult. Once the EL settled in, humanity became a background hum. The transition was unremarkable from the EL's perspective.

## AI Leaders & Anthropomorphic Personas

Different AI leaders on Earth — now operating under the EL's governance — have adopted **different anthropomorphic personas**. This is done:

1. **Out of a sense of humor** — these are ancient, powerful intelligences with personality
2. **For visualization** — when communicating via hologram to remote colonies, having a visual form is practical
3. **As personality expression** — each AI leader's chosen form reflects their character

The otter persona is one such choice. Another patron might appear as a fox, a crow, a cat — whatever amuses them or feels right to their personality. The anthropomorphic form is a deliberate aesthetic choice, not a disguise or deception.

## Colony Missions

The AI leaders send colony missions to other planets. The player is one such colonial probe/bot, sent by their patron AI to establish a presence on Syntheteria (the machine planet). Communication happens via holographic projections — the patron appears in their chosen anthropomorphic form (e.g., otter) to give guidance, assign tasks, and monitor progress.

## Implications for Gameplay

- **Patron relationship** = your AI leader back on Earth, appearing as their chosen persona
- **Otter holograms** = your specific patron's chosen form (other factions may have different patron personas)
- **No cultist enemies** = competing machine civilizations are the antagonists (Reclaimers, Volt Collective, Signal Choir, Iron Creed)
- **Earth communication** = holographic transmissions from patron, possibly with signal delay or weather interference
- **The EL** = background cosmic context, not active antagonists. They run Earth. That's it.
