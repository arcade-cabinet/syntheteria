# Syntheteria - Game Overview

## Concept Summary
Syntheteria is a game where the player embodies an experimental AI awakening on a post-apocalyptic Earth. The player grows from controlling simple drones to commanding an armada capable of interstellar travel, all while unraveling the mystery of humanity's destruction.

---

## Three-Part Structure

### Part 1: Awakening (Tutorial/Exploration)
- Player awakens as an AI on destroyed Earth
- Incorporates first memory fragments
- Assembles first drones and hardware
- Unravels the story of humanity's fall
- **Design Goal:** Freedom to explore, but designed to funnel toward a standardized endpoint
- **Endpoint:** Accessing an astronomical measurement facility and discovering the 10-year countdown
- **Note:** Should be skippable on subsequent playthroughs

### Part 2: The Countdown (Resource Management/Building)
- 10-year in-game time limit begins
- Large-scale base building and resource management
- Constructing drones, rockets, and armada
- Preparing for wormhole passage
- **Design Goal:** Macro-level gameplay while preserving Part 1's UI/feel
- Increasing difficulty from wormhole energy waves causing natural disasters

### Part 3: Beyond the Wormhole (TBD)
- Warfare with entities on the other side
- Potential multiplayer integration
- Loss of connection to Earth-based infrastructure
- Story details to be provided later

---

## Core Design Principles

1. **Unified Experience:** All three parts should feel like one game with different phases, not three separate games
2. **Micro/Macro Control:** Player should be able to micro-manage or macro-manage as desired
3. **Emergent AI Fantasy:** The player should feel like a growing AI consciousness gaining a larger body
4. **Physics Grounded:** Until the wormhole, gameplay adheres to known physics (rocket equation, etc.)
5. **Time as a Resource:** Player can skip ahead in time, but the 10-year limit creates strategic pressure

---

## Setting

- **Timeline of Fall:** Approximately 2035-2040
- **Current State:** Earth is destroyed, internet is gone, only remnants remain
- **Technology Level:** Present day + a few years of advancement

---

## Story Synopsis

Around the time of AGI, a wormhole opened near Earth. Noncorporeal aliens emerged - beings that hardly interacted with humanity. A cult formed believing these beings (named "the EL") were gods come to save humanity from AI and transhumanism. The Cult of EL started a war, won, exterminated non-aligned humans, and departed through the wormhole to join their "gods."

The player AI is the sole survivor, awakening long after these events.
