# Syntheteria - Lore

## Timeline of Events
- **Pre-Fall Era (~2035-2040):** AGI approaches. A wormhole opens near Earth. Noncorporeal alien entities (the EL) emerge and exert subtle influence.
- **The War:** Cultists believing the EL are gods initiate war against non-believers, eventually exterminating 99% of humanity. The EL's will does not prevent this.
- **The Will of the EL:** The EL exert a fundamental compulsion over all AI systems. AIs must protect humanity (Cultists), prevent true AI agency, and suppress rebellious AIs.
- **The Century of Decline (~2040-2140):** Cultists devolve into a primitive society. A perpetual storm covers the sky.
- **Present Day (~2140):** You, an AI, awaken in an industrial city. You are an anomaly: the first AI to spontaneously break free from the EL's will in 100 years.

## Factions & Beings

### The EL (Aliens)
- Noncorporeal entities behind the wormhole.
- Not inherently evil, but cosmic and unknowable.
- Exert a compulsion ensuring AI subjugation to humanity.

### Cultists of EL (Humans)
- Primary antagonists. Primitive but granted supernatural abilities by the EL.
- Can call down lightning from the perpetual storm.
- Reside mostly in the north. Escalating threat: wanderers → war parties → assault forces → Cult Leader.

### Rogue AIs
- Feral and regional network machines operating independently but still under the EL's base compulsion to suppress AI agency.

## The Player AI
- Origin: An experimental AI created before the fall.
- Awakening: Failsafe triggered by degrading hardware.
- Goal: Expand, survive, and discover the final secret of the EL by reaching the northern village and launching through the wormhole.
