# Syntheteria - Part 1: Awakening

## Overview

Part 1 serves as the introduction, tutorial, and story foundation. The player awakens as an AI, learns the core mechanics, and uncovers the history of humanity's fall.

---

## Opening Sequence

### The Awakening (Cutscene)
1. Abstract void - player exists as pure awareness
2. Gradual emergence into awareness of existing within a network
3. Text scrawl narration accompanies the awakening
4. Glitchy, corrupted visual aesthetic during this sequence
5. Corruption clears as player stabilizes

### First Moment of Control
- Player discovers hardware they can network with
- First action: Connecting to/activating that first piece of hardware
- Tutorial begins organically from this point

---

## The World State

### Environment at Game Start (~2140)
- **100 years since humanity's fall**
- Nature has reclaimed civilization:
  - Cities overgrown with vegetation
  - Ruins partially collapsed, covered in plants
  - Wildlife has repopulated the land
- **BUT:** The dying has begun
  - Wormhole radiation is now killing plants
  - Wildlife is dying with the plants
  - This decay will accelerate through Part 2

### What Remains
- Crumbling infrastructure, partially functional
- Server racks and data centers (memory sources)
- Dormant robots and drones (can be activated)
- Manufacturing facilities (need repair/power)
- The internet is gone - only local networks remain

---

## Progression Through Part 1

### Core Activities
1. **Activate first drone** - gain mobility
2. **Explore the environment** - discover locations
3. **Find and incorporate memory** - gain story + capabilities
4. **Activate more hardware** - expand your presence
5. **Learn automation** - prepare for scaling up
6. **Discover facilities** - unlock manufacturing capability
7. **Piece together the story** - understand the fall

### Tutorial Elements
Part 1 teaches the player:
- [ ] How to network with and control hardware
- [ ] How to navigate using the mind space interface
- [ ] How to focus on individual drones for direct control
- [ ] How to set up automation for drones
- [ ] How memory works (finding, incorporating, story fragments)
- [ ] How the four constraints affect operations
- [ ] Basic resource gathering and manufacturing

### Story Beats
Through memory fragments, the player learns:
- [ ] The emergence of AGI
- [ ] The opening of the wormhole
- [ ] The arrival of the EL
- [ ] The rise of the Cult of EL
- [ ] The war and extermination
- [ ] The exodus through the wormhole
- [ ] The player AI's origin and purpose

---

## Part 1 Endpoint

### Requirements to Complete Part 1
All three must be satisfied:

1. **Story Progression**
   - Key story beats unlocked through memory
   - Player has the full history of the fall

2. **Memory Threshold**
   - Sufficient memory incorporated
   - Enables capabilities needed for Part 2

3. **Capabilities/Facilities**
   - Specific facilities under control
   - Key capabilities unlocked
   - **[TBD]:** Exact list of required facilities/capabilities

### Transition to Part 2
- Game enables the astronomical facility
- Guides player to it if not already discovered
- Player uses facility and discovers:
  - The wormhole is emitting destructive radiation
  - Radiation intensity is increasing
  - **10 years until complete surface destruction**
- The countdown begins
- Part 2 officially starts

---

## Part 1 Replayability

### Skip Option
- After completing Part 1 once, players can skip on subsequent playthroughs
- Skip places player at the standardized Part 1 endpoint
- Same facilities, capabilities, and story knowledge
- Allows players to go directly to Part 2 resource management
